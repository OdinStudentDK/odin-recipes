Homemade Nuggets

How do I make homemade nuggets?

A brief overview

  1. Cut the chicken breasts into nuggets.
  2. Mix the bread crumbs, cheese, and seasonings.
  3. Dip the nuggets in melted butter, then coat them in the bread crumb mixture.
  4. Bake until the chicken is brown and cooked through.

A few tips

  • You can make these chicken nuggets in your air fryer, just follow the recipe, but cut the cook time in half.
  • Use this recipe as a guide. You can flavor them however you like. I suggest adding cayenne pepper for spicy nuggets or leave out the seasonings all together for a plain version.
  • I recommend flipping the nuggets halfway through the cook time, as one side may get crispier than the other.

Ingredients

  • 3 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup Italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup butter, melted

Directions

STEP 1

Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).

STEP 2

Cut chicken breasts into 1 1/2-inch pieces. Mix the bread crumbs, Parmesan, basil, thyme, and salt together well in a medium bowl. Put melted butter in a bowl or dish for dipping.

STEP 3

Dip chicken pieces into the melted butter first, then coat with the bread crumb mixture. Place the well-coated chicken nuggets on a lightly greased cookie sheet in a single layer.

STEP 4

Bake nuggets in the preheated oven until browned and chicken is cooked through, about 20 minutes.
